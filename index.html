About us

People with ADHD can easily be misunderstood and often become isolated from their community, they more likely to suffer with poor mental health and lack the confidence to deal with everyday tasks. However with the right diagnosis, treatment and support ADHD can be a superpower. Welcome to our community for adults with ADHD.

Mission

  1. To help people with ADHD by creating a supportive community of peers.
  2. Connect people with specialist services.
  3. Educate the general public about ADHD.
  4. Provide workplace learning and development to make businesses more inclusive for people with ADHD.
  5. Create a community of ADHD influencers.
  6. Use our community for good.
